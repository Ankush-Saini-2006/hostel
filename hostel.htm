About the Hostel

Cauvery Bhawan is a well-maintained girls hostel at NIT Kurukshetra, providing a safe, clean, and disciplined environment for students.

Facilities

  • Single / Double / Triple Sitter Rooms
  • Mess Facility (Hygienic Food)
  • Laundry Facility
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi
  • 24×7 Security
  • Daily Attendance

Hostel Rules

  1. Electrical appliances are not allowed.
  2. Loud music is strictly prohibited.
  3. Only female guardians are allowed inside.
  4. Students must follow discipline & cleanliness.
